What it is

A juicy fighter you can play on the toilet.

5–15 min runs

Roguelike loop. Pick a fighter, fight 8 waves, finish the boss, unlock more. Phone-friendly snack sessions.

Cinematic finishers

Drop an enemy below 15% HP and the Fruitality lands — Cinemachine swoops, time freezes, juice geyser. Auto-clipped to share.

Six fruit warriors

Each with their own juice color. Watermelon bleeds red. Banana bleeds gold. Durian stinks. Coconut breaks open like milk.

Cross-platform

iOS, Android, Mac, Steam — single build pipeline. Free-to-play, cosmetic-only IAP. No pay-to-win. Ever.
